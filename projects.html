Manage App

This application will help user manager their restaurant easier. This application have 3 version for 3 member at restaurant: A waiter, a cashier and a manager. A waiter will have a list of table at restaurant. He will choose table to serve and get the order information of customer. Then all informations will sent to cashier. Cashier will receive an information of food and then print at 2 version: one for customer with the receipt and the other for the cook to make a food. At manager page will screen all information about restaurnt such as: income, material, employee.
